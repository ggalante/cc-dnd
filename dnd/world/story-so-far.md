## Pre-Session

The world of Aldenmere has enjoyed fifty years of peace since the Brightblade Company sealed away the Void King. Magic is mundane. Monsters are mostly gone. Dungeons have been converted to grain storage and tourist attractions. Nobody carries a sword unless they're in a reenactment.

Four unlikely individuals have been hired by Aldous Finch, steward to the largely indifferent Regional Lord Harwick, to investigate why the village of Mosswick has gone quiet. No cheese tribute. No travelers. Two weeks of silence.

The party:
- **Sir Reginald Pompsworth III**, a paladin whose entire order dissolved because there was nothing left to be holy about, who has spent fifteen years waiting for evil to return so he can be useful
- **Zyx**, a 400-year-old elven archmage who has defeated two previous Great Evils and is deeply, vocally exhausted by the prospect of a third
- **Midge Thistlewick**, a halfling rogue of enormous ambition and almost no self-awareness, absolutely certain this is finally her moment of destiny
- **Grunka**, a half-orc barbarian of terrifying combat ability and surprising emotional intelligence, who watched something dark take her tribe twenty years ago and has been waiting for the rest of the world to catch up

They have their gold, their gear, and a vague set of directions. Mosswick is two days north, through the Ashwood.

The adventure has not yet begun.
