## Opinions of Party Members

**Reginald**: He called her "Miss Thistlewick" on their first meeting. Nobody has done that in twelve years. She has been thinking about this more than she wants to admit. He is very earnest. She took credit for one of his ideas (it was a small idea) and he wrote her a note. She framed the note. She is not examining why she framed the note.

**Zyx**: Infuriating. He clearly knows everything. He clearly finds her mildly amusing (she can tell; she is very good at reading people). He refuses to confirm any of this. She has called him "Old Z" three times and he has not responded to it once, which she has decided counts as acceptance. She wants his approval and will not admit this to anyone, including her journal. (She is writing it in her journal right now.)

**Grunka**: She loves Grunka, she has decided. Grunka listens. Grunka gives her full undivided attention. The fact that the attention is usually focused on trying to get Midge to "process underlying anxieties" is a minor detail. Grunka offered her tea at dinner and she accepted and it was actually quite good and she felt something she is filing under "not relevant."
